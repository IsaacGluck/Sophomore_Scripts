Music

Music has been a large part of my life ever since I was a young lad. At the young age of 3, I was enrolled in the Suzuki violin program. I rose through the ranks of Suzuki, playing hard and practicing digilently every day for 9 years. Suzuki however, did not last for ever and when I was 12 I moved on to private lessons with private teachers where I had more freedom to experiment and grow as a violinist.

At the age of 14 I began teaching myself guitar, a hobby that I have kept up and that has helped my musical ability tremedously.

Sports

Like, music, sports have also always been a large part of my life. From when I was a little kid, my dad would take me to the park to play a variety of sports from soccer, to football, to basketball. The sport that stuck however was soccer. Over the years I played in West Side Soccer League(WSSL) but as I got older I moved onto Manhattan Soccer Club (MSC), a travel soccer club. Here I played on some of the best premiere teams in the state. Last year however (2011), life threw me a curve ball as I injured my back seriously. Whether or not I will need surgery is still unkown but I have not been active for over a year and I will likely never play at the premiere level again. The initial effect of this loss was devestating, however, I quickly realized that I was never going to play proffesionaly, I had to stop at some point. This tragedy also lead to a large free up of my time which allowed me to focus on other important aspects of my life like music and school.

School

My real school experience began in elementary school when I attended the Alfred E. Smith School (P.S. 163 ) In 5th grade I was accepted into the DELTA program at M.S. 54 . Finally, in 8th grade I was accepted to Stuyvesant Highschool where I am currently enrolled. Throughout school I have never really had a favorite subject, it has really depended on the teacher. Whenever I have a charismatic, intelligent and caring teacher, the subject they teach becomes my favorite while I'm in their class. Recently however, if I had to choose a subject I would lean towards either Biology, Chemistry or Computer Science. Throughout middle and highschool I have participated in many clubs and teams. In middle school, I was point guard on the Booker T. Raiders (the basketball team) as well as a part of the Model UN club. In high school, I was on the soccer team, the track team, and a part of the Model UN club and The Spectator (the school newspaper). Currently I am the executive producer of SING!, the inter-grade competition to put on a killer musical/show in a month time. Every club I have been in as well as every school I have attended have helped me to learn and grow.
